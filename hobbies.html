Hobbies

Basketball

Basketball has been a huge part of my life. It keeps me active, teaches teamwork, and sharpens my decision-making. I play pickup games at the RSOP center and try to practice shooting several times a week.

Personal stories

One of my favorite memories was during middle school basketball where we came back from a 10-point deficit in the fourth quarter. In fact, I was the one who made the game winner. That win taught me to be resilient, to be a leader, and always stay humble. This is just one of many personal stories I have. Feel free to reach out to me using my information given for more details!

Why it matters

Basketball keeps me balanced, helps with stress management, and gives me community away from schoolwork. I believe strongly in balancing work life, school life, and personal life. Basketball is one of the few things that allows me to escape from reality and be myself.

Golf

Golf is my outdoor escape. I enjoy the focus required for the short game and the satisfaction of a well-executed round. I play several local courses in the summer and practice at the range when I can.

Personal stories

I remember playing an early morning nine with a professor who gave me tips about course management — the experience reinforced how sports can connect you with people across disciplines.

Why it matters

Golf provides patience and discipline — qualities that translate well into finance and professional life.
